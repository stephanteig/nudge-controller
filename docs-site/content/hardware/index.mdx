# Hardware

The physical build is a single PCB plus a case. The board carries:

- a **nice!nano v2** socket (MCU + BLE + LiPo charger),
- a **21-key matrix** (3 rows × 7 columns) of MX/Choc switches with per-key diodes,
- an **Alps EC11K** rotary encoder with two 10 kΩ pull-ups and a push-switch,
- a **JST-PH 2.0 mm** LiPo connector and an inline power switch.

## Hardware specs

- **MCU:** nice!nano v2 (nRF52840), Pro Micro pinout
- **Encoder:** Alps EC11K, 24 PPR, metal shaft, ~100k-cycle life
- **Matrix:** 21 keys, 3 rows × 7 columns, 1N4148 per key
- **Battery:** 3.7 V 2000 mAh LiPo, charged by the nice!nano's onboard BQ24072
- **Wireless:** Bluetooth 5.0, up to 5 paired host profiles
